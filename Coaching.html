GRIT Coaching Program Outline for Families Facing Grief and Loss


Introduction: The GRIT Coaching Program (Grief, Recovery, Illness, and Trauma) is dedicated to helping families navigate the challenges of grief and loss. We have tailored four distinct programs for the core members of the family—moms, dads, siblings, and the family unit as a whole.

Each program takes a holistic approach, focusing on mental, physical, nutritional, and spiritual well-being. Below is the detailed structure for each program.

For Moms

My name is Allene, and I am a health and wellness coach with a deep passion for helping women navigate life’s most difficult challenges. Having gone through my own journey of healing from trauma and grief, I understand how isolating and overwhelming the process can feel. That’s why I founded this company—to offer moms a space where they can heal, connect with others who understand their pain, and celebrate their strength as they move forward. Through my GRIT program (grief, recovery, illness, and trauma), and our transformative retreats, I guide women to find peace and renewal, empowering them to live a life of joy and self-love.”


• Mission: A nurturing, safe space for women to heal emotionally, physically, and spiritually.
• Vision: A world where women experiencing trauma, grief, and illness can find support and healing.
• Goals: To offer life-changing retreats that build community, provide expert wellness support, and facilitate deep emotional recovery.
• Objectives: Regular retreats, expert-led sessions, community-building post-retreat resources.
• Why: A deeply personal connection to trauma and healing.
• Message: A compassionate and empathetic offer to moms seeking renewal and recovery..

Cultivated Moms

“Cultivating Strength, Nourishing Your Spirit.”

Designed for moms who often carry the emotional burden of family grief. This program emphasizes self-care, emotional resilience, and personal growth through tailored wellness practices.

Cost: $800 (5-day program), includes all coaching 1-1 sessions, materials, nutritional plan as well an exercise
10 day plan

Resilient Dads

 “Building Strength, Anchoring the Family.”

This program supports fathers who feel the weight of holding their families together through grief. It focuses on balancing emotional vulnerability with strength, while fostering connection with their children and spouse.

Cost: $750 (5-day program), includes all materials, and coaching sessions and fitness support

Enriched Siblings

“Embracing Grief, Growing Together.”

Unique supportive program specifically for siblings to help them express their grief, understand their feelings, and find their unique place in the family during times of loss.

Cost: $600 (4-day program), includes all activities, healthy nutritional snack , and workbooks.

Connected Families

 “Healing Together, Growing Stronger.”

A comprehensive program that brings together the entire family, helping them heal as a unit. It focuses on communication, shared experiences, and finding strength in their collective grief.

Cost: $1,500 (5-day program), includes family meals, group coaching sessions and wellness practices, material
